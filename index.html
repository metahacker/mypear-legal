Overview

MyPear ("we", "our", "us") provides a personal AI assistant service ("PEAR") that communicates with you via SMS, email, voice, and our mobile/web applications. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Communications: Messages you send to and receive from your PEAR
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via tokenization; we never store full card numbers

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI assistant services
  • To send you messages, reminders, and notifications you request
  • To process payments for services
  • To communicate important updates about our service

SMS Messaging

Program Name: MyPear AI Assistant

Message Frequency: Varies based on your usage. You control when your PEAR contacts you.

Message and Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

Opt-Out: Text STOP at any time to stop receiving SMS messages.

Help: Text HELP for assistance or contact support@mypear.ai

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (cloud hosting, payment processing, SMS delivery)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. Sensitive operations (payments, credentials) are only performed through our secure app, never via SMS or email.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting support@mypear.ai.
